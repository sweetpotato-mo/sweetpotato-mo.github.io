Introduction to Computer

Definition

A computer is an electronic device that receives data, processes it and gives meaningful information as output. In this chapter we go about the basics of a computer and get to know its parts better. To start with, a very generic picture of a desktop computer is given below.

Parts of a Computer

A computer has many parts that work together to give the required output. Some of the parts of a computer are :

  • CPU
  • Monitor
  • Keyboard
  • Mouse
  • Speakers
  • Printer
  • Scanner
  • Joystick
  • MIC
  • Camera, and many more
